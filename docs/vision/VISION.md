# Josephine — Product Vision

## Elevator Pitch

For restaurant owners and managers, **Josephine** is an AI-powered operations platform that transforms raw POS data into actionable intelligence. Unlike manual spreadsheets or basic POS dashboards, Josephine provides predictive forecasting, automated cost control, and AI-assisted decision making — all from a single connection to your existing POS.

## Why Josephine Exists

Restaurants run on razor-thin margins (5-15% net profit). The difference between a profitable restaurant and one that's bleeding money comes down to three things:

1. **Knowing your numbers** — most restaurants don't know their real food cost, labour cost, or prime cost until the accountant tells them weeks later
2. **Forecasting demand** — over-ordering food = waste, under-staffing = bad service
3. **Acting on data** — insights are worthless without recommended actions

Josephine solves all three by connecting to the data restaurant already generate (POS, inventory, timesheets) and turning it into real-time intelligence + AI recommendations.

## Target Personas

### 1. Restaurant Owner / Operator
- **Pain**: No real-time visibility into profitability. Relies on accountant for P&L once a month.
- **Want**: Dashboard that shows today's GP%, prime cost, and sales velocity
- **Josephine**: Dashboard, InstantPL, Control Tower

### 2. Kitchen Manager / Head Chef
- **Pain**: Recipe costing is manual. No idea of actual vs theoretical food cost.
- **Want**: Automated recipe costing, prep lists based on forecasts, waste tracking
- **Josephine**: Recipes, Inventory, PrepList, Waste, Escandallos

### 3. FOH / General Manager
- **Pain**: Scheduling is guesswork. Labour cost spikes during slow periods.
- **Want**: Demand-based scheduling, real-time labour cost %, shift management
- **Josephine**: Scheduling, Labour, Workforce, StaffClock

### 4. Multi-Unit Operator
- **Pain**: Can't compare locations. Relies on individual managers' reports.
- **Want**: Consolidated view, cross-location benchmarking, anomaly detection
- **Josephine**: Multi-location support, future: Location Comparison view

## Core Value Propositions

| # | Value Prop | How |
|---|-----------|-----|
| 1 | **Connect → Intelligence** | OAuth POS integration → instant dashboards (zero manual data entry) |
| 2 | **AI Forecasting** | Ensemble Prophet + XGBoost → predict sales, labour needs, stock requirements |
| 3 | **Real-Time Cost Visibility** | Know food cost and GP% every day, not once a month |
| 4 | **Labour Optimization** | Forecast-driven scheduling → right people at the right time |
| 5 | **AI Assistant** | Josephine Chat → ask questions about your business in natural language |

## North Star

> **"Every restaurant decision, powered by data and AI"**

## Design Philosophy

- **Feels premium** — restaurant owners deserve beautiful tools, not spreadsheets
- **AI-first** — every screen should have an AI insight or recommendation
- **Mobile-ready** — managers are never at a desk
- **Demo-friendly** — the demo must sell the product in 2 minutes
